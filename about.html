The blog and its author

I hope to share the best of my knowledge and ideas with others in desire to begin a conversation that can be used for the betterment of all of us.
Hey there! My name is Yana, and this blog is a collection of answers to the questions I have come across in my life. I find my answers in books, conversations, internet videos and articles, and simply pondering over things myself. After trying different solutions and finding what works for me, I decided that sharing this knowledge could be of use to someone else. And so here we are!
I am originally from Saint-Petersburg, Russia. I lived there until I was about 10, when my mom and I decided to move to Belarus for a short period of time. After about a year, we moved to the United States. I have lived in Florida since and have traveled in many other countries. I believe that traveling is one of the best ways to find answers to important life questions.
I attended college to study entrepreneurship, but have yet to start my own real business. I have gained many different skills over my life. I am particularly interested in photography, web design, and marketing. I am now studying web development as it has always been one of my goals to learn programming.
