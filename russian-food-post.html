3 Russian dishes you need to try

by Yana Markova

April 30th, 2021
If you are curious about Russian culture, then these dishes are a must for you to explore.

1. The Russian Salad, or Oliv’e

This salad is a traditional dish or all Russian celebrations - New Years, birthdays, homecomings, and more. It is very filling and high in carbs, which makes it extremely satisfying. The dish usually includes potatoes, carrots, eggs, onion, cucumbers or pickles, peas, and mayonnaise. It’s very cheap and easy to make, which might be the main reason why it is such a staple in Russian cuisine.

2. Beef Stroganoff

Another caloric dish made entire o meat with the most amazing sauce of sour cream and spices. It’s a ‘man’ dish, as a Russian would see it. The flavor is very unique and very satisfying. It is also fun to make, but does take some effort on the chef’s part, since the meat needs to be cut in thin strips and pre-cooked before included in the dish. O course, it can also be made the simple way, if you mind the extra effort.

3. Russian cutlets

Made in practically every Russian family and every grandmother’s treat are cutlets - basically meatballs that are eaten as a meat portion of a dish. The ground meat is generally mixed with white bread dipped in milk, onions, and spices and then fried. They are a childhood memory for Russians, and a wonderful dish for others.

Find the recipes and try them out, you won’t regret giving these dishes a chance!
